# SmartHomeEnergyAdvisorAgent

## AI-Powered Personalized Energy Management System

**Platform:** IBM Cloud
**Technology:** IBM watsonx Orchestrate

---

## Overview

The **Smart Home Energy Advisor Agent** is an AI-powered virtual energy consultant that continuously monitors household electricity consumption, analyzes usage patterns, predicts electricity bills, and provides personalized recommendations to optimize energy usage.

The agent leverages a knowledge base containing smart meter data, appliance usage logs, and user preferences to generate actionable insights and energy-saving recommendations. By transforming raw electricity consumption data into meaningful information, the system helps households make informed decisions, reduce energy wastage, and lower electricity costs.

---

## Problem Statement

The Challenge
Rising electricity demand and energy costs make it difficult for households to understand how their daily habits impact power consumption. Most users only receive monthly bills, which provide limited insight into appliance-level energy usage. As a result, people often waste electricity unknowingly, highlighting the need for an AI-powered system that analyzes usage patterns and provides actionable energy-saving recommendations.
The Objective
Build an AI-powered Smart Home Energy Advisor Agent that analyzes household electricity consumption data and provides intelligent recommendations to optimize energy usage.
1.	Energy Consumption Analysis – Analyze household electricity usage patterns from smart meters or appliance usage logs.
2.	Personalized Energy Recommendations – Suggest energy-saving actions based on the user's consumption behavior and daily routines.
3.	Appliance Usage Insights – Identify appliances that consume the most electricity and recommend optimal usage strategies.
4.	Electricity Bill Estimation – Predict monthly electricity bills based on current usage patterns and tariff structures.
5.	Energy Efficiency Suggestions – Recommend simple practices such as reducing peak-hour usage, scheduling heavy appliances, or upgrading to energy-efficient devices.

---

## Solution

The **Smart Home Energy Advisor Agent** acts as an intelligent energy advisor that continuously learns from household energy consumption data and provides personalized recommendations to improve energy efficiency.

The agent performs the following functions:

* Monitors household electricity consumption continuously
* Analyzes energy usage patterns and trends
* Identifies high-energy-consuming appliances
* Predicts monthly electricity bills
* Provides personalized energy-saving recommendations
* Generates alerts and proactive suggestions
* Helps users optimize energy usage and reduce costs

## Knowledge Base

The agent uses the following knowledge sources:

### 1. Smart Meter Data

* Timestamp
* Total Energy Consumed (kWh)
* Hourly Consumption
* Daily Consumption
* Peak and Off-Peak Usage

### 2. Appliance Usage Logs

* Appliance Name
* Power Rating (W/kW)
* Usage Duration
* Frequency of Usage

### 3. User Inputs

* Number of Family Members
* Monthly Electricity Budget
* Preferred Comfort Settings
* Working Schedule
* Energy-Saving Preferences
